🧠 Swag Strategy Framework

1. Signature Self-Promo Swag

  • Use seasonal or themed drops as conversation starters.
  • Focus on layered experiences.
  • Align with brand tone: design forward, cheeky, thoughtful.

2. Client Gifting & Onboarding

  • Curated swag that wows new clients and nurtures relationships.
  • Co-brand subtly and include branded touchpoints.

3. Team Pride & Industry Presence

  • Equip staff with merch that’s wearable and share worthy.
  • Drop limited edition items during high profile seasons.

4. Giveaway & Conference Swag

  • Lead the category with standout swag.
  • Use QR codes creatively and with intention.
