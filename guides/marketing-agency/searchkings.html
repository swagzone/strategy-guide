🧱 Swag Strategy Framework

1. Client Loyalty & Engagement

  • Swag reflects performance and partnership—you’re their marketing engine.
  • Combine practical tech items with surprise-and-delight gestures.
  • Embed QR/NFC codes for reports, tips, or case studies.

2. Team Culture & Retention

  • Build unity across Toronto, Burnaby, and remote teams.
  • Celebrate milestones and roles (e.g. PPC Ninjas, SEO Legends).
  • Design gear that works for Zoom or IRL wear.

3. Event & Partner Brand Visibility

  • Go bold and useful at expos and partner events.
  • Focus on branded tools and wearables with repeat exposure.
  • Co-brand exclusives with clients or Google (limited = prestige).

4. Giveaway & Conference Swag

  • Fun, functional, and conversation-worthy swag only.
  • Highlight SearchKings' performance, franchise expertise, Google Premier status.
  • Smart packaging and contact-driven design for easy follow-up.
