💡 Implementation Tips

📦 Use Tiered Gifting Tracks
Create tracks for “onboarding,” “referral,” and “retreat” swag. This simplifies planning while letting you scale reward tiers with clarity and purpose.
📸 Gamify Content With Swag Challenges
Run mini-challenges that involve posting swag use with hashtags. It boosts brand visibility, reinforces community, and makes founders part of the momentum.
🛠️ Keep Coaches Equipped and Aligned
Send quarterly care kits to coaches with updated gear and collateral. Consistency in delivery equals consistency in brand—and results.
