Implementation Tips

⚛️ Branded Intelligence Over Logo Bombs
Swag is a communication tool. Let your items feel like artifacts from the lab, the launchpad, or the boardroom—not loud promo pieces. Subtle > shouty.
🌍 Sustainability Is Your Signature
Bamboo, recycled PET, and upcycled materials aren’t just nice—they align perfectly with your mission. Bonus: they look and feel better than plastic giveaways.
🧪 Small Batch, Big Impact
Skip bulk giveaways. Go for meaningful drops of 25–50 units that feel earned, premium, and purposeful—just like your work.
🔄 Turn Swag Into a Touchpoint
Add NFC/QR cards that open whitepapers, slide decks, or investor intros. Make your swag a conversation extender, not a one-time gesture.
