Implementation Tips

🎁 Build Moments of Surprise
Create delightful brand memories. Include “Wellness Nap Kits” or unexpected extras in customer shipments or loyalty programs.
🔄 Rotate Themes Seasonally
Align swag with product lines. Switch focus quarterly—Liberator (romantic), Avana (wellness), Jaxx (lifestyle).
📦 Leverage Packaging as Brand Channel
Maximize every unboxing. Add sensory items like rollers or spa bands to elevate the D2C experience.
🤝 Coordinate Across Teams
Apparel = internal alignment. Use softshells and cardigans to present a cohesive front at expos, retail, or in content.
🌱 Tie to Sustainability
Let values show. Reinforce Luvu’s domestic production and sustainable packaging story through eco-aligned swag.
