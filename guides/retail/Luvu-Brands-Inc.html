Implementation Tips

🎨 Design for Identity
Let swag express your incubator’s voice. From founder mottos to product-born rituals, let each piece deepen the story.
📦 Box With Intention
Make the unboxing cinematic. Use color, scent, sound (QR codes), and tactility to elevate perception.
🎙️ Embed Micro-Stories
Add founder voice notes, napkin sketch reprints, or vision cards. Let people hear, touch, or read your origin moments.
🔥 Limit It
Use drops and scarcity. “Only 75 Founder Tokens.” Make swag feel earned, not given.
👁️‍🗨️ Use It In Retail
Swag = showroom styling props. Use coasters, mirrors, or zines as visual assets in your branded spaces.
