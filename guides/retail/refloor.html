🌟 Swag Strategy Framework

1. Field Sales & Installer Identity

  • Branded gear for daily wear builds team unity and trust
  • Use premium items as recruitment or milestone recognition tools
  • Include leave-behind swag to extend homeowner impression

2. Customer Trust & Loyalty

  • Create “Wow” moments post-installation with thank-you kits
  • Include QR-linked care tips or review links on swag
  • Position Refloor as polished, thoughtful, and service-first

3. Internal Team Culture

  • Celebrate top performers with tiered swag bundles
  • Use apparel and desk gear to reinforce pride in brand
  • Swag up onboarding to boost day-one engagement

4. Giveaway & Conference Swag

  • Prioritize practical items homeowners will keep
  • Include QR or NFC-enabled info (e.g., financing, gallery, booking)
  • Drive booth traffic with tactile, value-add swag
