Implementation Tips

🧘 Onboarding that feels sacred
Give the kit at first check-in and include a short “first-week ritual” card: 1 breathwork session, 1 sound bath, 1 hot/cold—plus a QR to book. This sparks immediate usage and forms habit loops tied to your modalities.
🏷️ Build the patch journey
Pre-design a 24-month arc (3/6/12/18/24) with element motifs. Announce each drop on socials and a studio wall tracker. Members love a visible arc from “New to Ritual” → “Keeper of the Flame.”
🛒 Keep the store tiny & profitable
Cap SKUs to ~12 with clear bundles (Onboarding Kit, Wet-Zone Trio). Use shelf-talkers with one-line benefits + QR to care/warranty. Reorder monthly using a simple sell-through sheet.
📸 Content moments = sales moments
Place a small photo spot by the plunge exit with a discrete “I Did the Dip” placard and pins on display—people snap, tag, and ask where to buy.
🚿 Designed for wet zones
Prioritize quick-dry and non-slip (slides, towels, pouches). Add laundry symbols to robe and towel labels to reduce wear and maintain spa feel.
🤝 Partner bundles
Bundle guest passes with giftables (roller + mug + pass) for member birthdays or corporate retreats; include a sticker/patch to seed the loyalty story.
