Implementation Tips

💬 Use Swag as a Talent Retention Tool
Reward and remind. Field workers often feel overlooked—showing appreciation with branded gear can cement loyalty and increase job satisfaction.
🎯 Give Partners Swag They’ll Actually Use
Think desks, meetings, and repeat visibility. Swag that lives on their desk reminds them of your professionalism every day.
🎉 Gamify Hiring Events
Add small wins. Use sticker packs, spin wheels, or mini-swag bundles as incentives to drive booth engagement and collect leads.
🚚 Build Regional Consistency Across Offices
Centralize core swag kits (e.g., recruiter polos, client folders) so each Argus office delivers a uniform brand experience.
📦 Bundle by Use Case
Starter kits, thank-you kits, reactivation kits. Pre-assembled swag kits make your outreach efficient and emotionally resonant.
