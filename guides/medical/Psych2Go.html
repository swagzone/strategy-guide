Implementation Tips

💡 Tip 1 – Use Merch Drops to Spark Storytelling
Create themes. Use concepts like “Anxious But Thriving” or “My Therapist Likes This Mug” as monthly drop ideas that merge humor with heart.
🛒 Tip 2 – Optimize Storefront Navigation
Group items by vibe or goal (e.g., “Mood Lifters,” “Comfort Wear,” “Quiet Tools”) instead of just product types to drive emotional browsing.
🔁 Tip 3 – Encourage Repeat Buyers with Tiered Merch
Offer rewards like exclusive pins or digital zines after multiple purchases or fan referrals to turn casual shoppers into collectors.
🌱 Tip 4 – Empower Chapters with Custom Bundles
Design a “Starter Pack” for global community chapters: banner, plushie, notebooks, and leader patch. Makes launching feel official.
🤳 Tip 5 – Leverage UGC for Product Validation
Before launching a new plush, prompt your audience: “Which emotion should Psi express next?” It builds hype and locks in buy-in before release.
