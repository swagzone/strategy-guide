🏆 SWAG STRATEGY FRAMEWORK

1. Client Loyalty & Brand Trust

  • Deliver premium, clinic-ready items that signal professionalism
  • Embed UnisLink value props via QR-enabled or usage-based merch
  • Reinforce your tech-meets-service brand positioning

2. Internal Team Pride & Retention

  • Align swag with milestones and functional roles
  • Offer practical work-from-home and in-office swag
  • Use recognition merch to reward performance and reduce turnover

3. Medical Group Visibility

  • Distribute branded tools and leave-behinds to boost partner recall
  • Use tasteful, clinical swag at exec meetings and demos
  • Leverage co-branding with EHR/PM partners for shared exposure

4. Giveaway & Conference Swag

  • Prioritize booth-worthy items that feel helpful and professional
  • Use QR/URL swag to capture traffic and drive follow-ups
  • Include subtle healthcare humor or references for memorability
