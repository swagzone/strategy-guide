🎯 Swag Strategy Framework

1. Client Impact & Executive Workshops

  • Swag should mirror Get In Rhythm’s positioning: elite, strategic, and human-centered.
  • Use premium materials and intentional design to make every touchpoint feel like part of the experience.
  • Include workshop-specific items for note-taking, reflection, and post-session retention.

2. Team Identity & Remote Culture

  • With a distributed, high-performance team, swag builds cohesion and pride.
  • Use items that reflect both focus and flow—from creative gear to cozy WFH staples.
  • Think wearable culture, not just logo drops.

3. Brand Visibility & Thought Leadership

  • Leverage branded goods at Agile, DevOps, and Product conferences to stay memorable.
  • Use subtlety, sharp design, and interactivity to appeal to the tech-savvy crowd.

4. Giveaway & Conference Swag

  • Pick swag that sparks curiosity and post-event engagement.
  • Use QR/NFC items to drive traffic to resources like webinars or assessments.
