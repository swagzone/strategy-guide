Implementation Tips

🎯 Double Down on Exclusivity
Create concept-specific swag that only staff or early guests can earn—makes it feel part of something being built.

📦 Layer Presentation
Wrap each item like a launch moment—crinkle fill, info cards, custom banding—so it reflects Nova’s care in curation.

🌿 Reflect Brand Ethos
Choose elevated materials (stone, wood, tin, cotton) to match culinary aesthetics and sustainability values.

🔄 Mix Utility & Delight
Blend usable kitchen/work swag (aprons, clipboards) with emotional keepsakes (candles, flavor kits).

🗓️ Repeat for Key Touchpoints
Use swag across phases—pitch decks, tastings, launch, anniversary—so your consulting presence stays unforgettable.
