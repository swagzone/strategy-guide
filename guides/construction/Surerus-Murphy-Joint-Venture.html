Implementation Tips

🐢 Tip 1: Start Small, Scale Smartly
Begin with cost-effective items like mugs or safety bands—track what resonates with teams and partners, then amplify high-impact pieces next season.
🛠 Tip 2: Embedded Branding, Feel-Good Utility
Prioritize practical swag that’s useful onsite and reinforces your core values: safety, quality, and respect for community.
🎁 Tip 3: Local & Crafted for Connection
Whenever possible, choose locally sourced or handcrafted items for premium moments to deepen emotional resonance and community goodwill.
📏 Tip 4: Accommodate the Journey
Offer swag at multiple touchpoints: orientation, milestone celebrations, site tours—to reinforce identity and belonging at every stage.
🔁 Tip 5: Recycle for Reach
Ensure eco-conscious packaging or reclaimed material use aligns with SMJV’s sustainability and Net Zero 2050 messaging.
