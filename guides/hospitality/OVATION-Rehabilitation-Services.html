💡 Implementation Tips

🎯 Start with High-Retention Swag
Focus your budget first on daily-use items—badge reels, journals, lunch kits—that bring the brand into your team’s routine.
📦 Build Welcome Experiences, Not Just Kits
Use small details (notes, packaging, sensory items) to make your travel nurses or new partners feel truly welcomed into the Dzeel community.
🌐 Align Swag With Recruitment Cycles
Roll out themed items for contract completions, referrals, or travel anniversaries. It’s a cost-efficient way to drive retention and referrals.
🧪 Pilot Premium Concepts With Top Talent
Try the “Night Shift Kit” or “Gratitude Pouch” with 10–20 key nurses. Gather feedback, refine, and scale what works.
