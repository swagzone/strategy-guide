Implementation Tips

Swag by season: Roll out polos and hats in spring, towels and visors in summer, scorecard holders in fall—align with play cycles.
Social media swing: Include QR codes or hashtags with giveaways to encourage photo tagging—turn guests into brand ambassadors.
“Fore-thoughtful” packaging: Present items in linen bags, ribbon-wrapped boxes, or engraved kits for weddings and VIP guests.
