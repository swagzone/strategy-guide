🎯 Swag Strategy Framework

1. Guest-Centric Brand Experience

  • Make swag an extension of the service promise—elevated, useful, and share-worthy across properties.

2. Hospitality Team Engagement

  • Celebrate frontline excellence and loyalty through performance-based swag tiers and seasonal drops.

3. Portfolio-Wide Brand Alignment

  • Tie all properties back to NHS with subtle, cohesive merch—used across leadership summits, trainings, and vendor partnerships.

4. Giveaway & Conference Swag

  • Create trade show kits and guest experience items that reinforce your management leadership across hotel brands.

✅ Implementation Tips

📅 Swag by Role & Recognition: Create a seasonal drop calendar where each property gets themed swag by department (e.g., Housekeeping Week, Front Desk Fridays).
🧭 Brand-Inside-the-Brand Strategy: Use co-branded swag to tie Marriott, Hilton, and IHG properties visibly back to NHS during leadership summits or industry events.
👋 Recruiting & Onboarding Integration: Include NHS-branded folders, tumblers, and pins in onboarding kits for new GMs or hotel staff across your portfolio.
