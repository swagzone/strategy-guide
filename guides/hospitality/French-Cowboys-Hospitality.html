🚀 Implementation Tips

🎯 Build a “Mini-Moment” at Sales Meetings
Pair swag with storytelling. When gifting clients, include a handwritten card or QR code linking to a signature experience video or property tour.
🧵 Let Uniforms Be Identity-Driven
Give your team choices. Offer 2–3 style cuts (e.g. long sleeve, zip, polo) to accommodate varying preferences while keeping branding consistent.
📦 Bundle for Impact
Use branded boxes. Turn your giveaways into full kits with custom packaging—this makes even small items feel premium and purposeful.
🌱 Lean Into Sustainability
Select materials that echo your values. Cork, bamboo, and linen aren’t just trends—they align beautifully with eco-conscious hospitality.
📸 Create Shareable Moments
Encourage team and client selfies. Subtle co-branding or witty quotes on apparel and gifts make them Instagram-worthy and help drive organic visibility.
