🌟 Swag Strategy Framework

1. Client & Brand Partner Engagement

  • Reward integration partners with sleek, retail-quality gear
  • Gift swag that reinforces delivery transparency and efficiency
  • Make your brand tangible post-sale with smart leave-behinds

2. Internal Team Culture

  • Celebrate milestones with utility-first, on-brand merch

  • Use company values as design inspiration ("Precision," "Trust," "Speed")

3. Thought Leadership & Brand Visibility

  • Make swag a conversation starter about delivery innovation

  • Prioritize items that open conversations and drive follow-up
