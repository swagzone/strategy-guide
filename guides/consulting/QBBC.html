Implementation Tips

🎉 Celebrate Membership Visibly

Turn your directory into a physical network. From decals to pins, visible swag in store windows and at markets builds recognition and pride across neighborhoods.

📦 Create Tiered Gifting Systems

Not all gifts are created equal—and that’s a good thing. Use scalable merch tiers: small gifts for new partners, elevated items for sponsors, and exclusive concepts for donor circles.

📸 Design for Instagram Moments

Swag should feel photo-worthy. Use color, slogans, and symbolism (like progress flag motifs or quotes from queer icons) to drive shares, tags, and deeper identity alignment.

🫱🏽‍🫲🏾 Make Co-Branded Drops Easy

Let ally orgs sponsor swag. Offer select co-branded items with subtle dual-logo placement—mutual visibility without overpowering your message.
