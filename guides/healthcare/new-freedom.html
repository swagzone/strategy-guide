🌟 Swag Strategy Framework

1. Participant Empowerment & Belonging

  • Reinforce identity and pride with mission-tagged apparel
  • Provide practical gear for daily routines post-release
  • Encourage self-expression and program ownership

2. Peer Mentor & Staff Culture

  • Foster team unity with job-ready branded apparel
  • Recognize milestones with wearable achievement swag
  • Equip mobile staff with durable, mission-forward tools

3. Community & Donor Visibility

  • Use mission-tied merch in outreach and fundraising
  • Build trust through swag with values-first design
  • Amplify “second chances” with co-branded cause gear

4. Giveaway & Conference Swag

  • Provide useful, heart-led items with powerful messaging
  • Encourage event recall and deeper digital follow-up
  • Leverage wearable impact stories for booth engagement
