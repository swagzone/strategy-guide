2. Family & Client Connection

  • Thoughtful, home-friendly items that promote comfort and trust

✅ Implementation Tips

📦 Caregiver Welcome Kits: Onboard new hires with a branded bundle: vest, water bottle, badge lanyard, and socks. Sets the tone for pride and purpose.
🏨 Health Fair Booth Boosters: Use fridge clips, pill cases, and foam hearts to drive family engagement. Add QR cards linking to Americare’s intro video or care FAQs.
🌿 Referral Touch Kits: Deliver “Thank You” plant kits or pen + folder sets to partner clinics—co-brand if possible, and include service impact stats or case stories.
