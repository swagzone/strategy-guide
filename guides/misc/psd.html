🏗️ Ontario Client Conference 2026 | Swag & Experience Guide

Event: PSD Citywide Client Conferences (3x annually)
Audience: Municipal professionals – asset mgmt, public works, finance, building officials
Location: Ontario, Canada
Theme: Built In Ontario
Touchpoints: Welcome kits, booth activations, sessions, thank-you gifts
Each event is built to elevate client connection, municipal innovation, and platform learning with Ontario-inspired swag experiences.

🎪 Theme + Booth Experience

📍 Theme Ideas (Brand-Aligned + Municipal-Flavored)

  • “Blueprints for Better Communities” – Focused on planning, asset mgmt, GIS
  • “City Sync 2026” – For tech-forward sessions on data integration
  • “Public Works. Public Worth.” – For culture, infrastructure, finance
  • “Built In Ontario” – Celebrate local talent, solutions, partnerships
  • “Sustain the System” – Clean, urban design tied to sustainability in infrastructure

🎡 Booth Design Ideas

  • “Municipal Map Wall”: Guests pin their city on a large Ontario map + snap a Polaroid for a communal photo board
  • Live Asset Tagging Demo: Interactive screens or overlays showing PSD tools in action
  • City Builder Challenge Game: Guests "build a city" via puzzle or digital sim (parks, budgets, roads)

📋 Other Cool Ideas

  • QR Poster Boards: Banners at snack or coffee stations linking to PSD demos or features
  • “Swag for Feedback” Booth: Guests fill out quick surveys to earn tiered swag (e.g. stickers, mugs, hats)
  • Sticker Wall Exchange: Bring your city's sticker, get a PSD one back — builds culture and sharable content

✅ Implementation Tips

🧩 Use Layers of Swag: Intro items, in-session gifts, and thank-you surprises keep attendees engaged throughout.
📍 Localize Your Story: Highlight Ontario talent, cities, and data visuals on tote art, banners, and booth setups.
🎯 Track Engagement: Use QR cards and sticker checklists to see what booths and sessions drove the most interest.
