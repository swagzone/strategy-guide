🌟 SWAG STRATEGY FRAMEWORK

1. Insurance Partner Engagement

  • Utility items that stay in adjusters' cars or offices
  • Swag that signals reliability, speed, and professionalism
  • Subtle QR touchpoints for reorders or service booking

2. Field Tech Recognition

  • Uniform-ready gear that blends comfort with brand pride
  • Swag for milestones, hot zones, or safety challenges
  • “Dry & Restore Heroes” seasonal drops

3. Franchise & Brand Visibility

  • Standardized yet localized gear to unify the FRSTeam network
  • Mobile-advertising swag: vans, field bags, roadside touchpoints
  • Highlight eco-safe, tech-forward restoration through merch

4. Giveaway & Conference Swag

  • Keep swag functional for adjusters, memorable for booth visitors
  • Use humor + urgency: “Dry Fast. Recover Smart.”
  • Include QR/NFC touchpoints to book demos or refer claims
