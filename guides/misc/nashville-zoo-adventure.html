✅ Implementation Tips

🧭 Design an Experience, Not a Bag: Every item should tie to a moment, not just fill a tote. Swag becomes memory when it connects to action.
🌀 Stagger the Surprise: Reveal items at each zone, letting guests unlock new gifts as they roam.
🎨 Wear It, Frame It, Share It: Opt for wearable swag (vests, pins, bracelets) or framed Polaroids for lasting memories.
🤝 Let Sponsors Host, Not Just Brand: Give sponsors a narrative role with hosted giveaways, games, and take-home tokens.
🧁 Wrap with Emotion: The final carousel moment should be sweet, sentimental, and unforgettable.
