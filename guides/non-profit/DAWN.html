Implementation Tips

♿ Lead with access, not just branding

Choose products that reduce friction. Prioritize easy-open closures, comfortable grips, readable decoration, lighter weights, and soft-touch materials. The most successful DAWN swag will feel like it was selected with lived experience in mind.

🧡 Make usefulness the measure of success

Practicality creates retention. For this audience, the best swag is not flashy—it is the item someone reaches for again and again. Focus on hydration, organization, comfort, and event navigation over novelty.

🎨 Keep decoration calm and high-contrast

Readable branding feels more inclusive. Clean typography, strong contrast, and uncluttered layouts will make the products feel more accessible and more premium at the same time.

📦 Build kits around real moments

Think in use cases, not just categories. Workshop day, travel day, advocacy meeting, peer support gathering, or speaker thank-you kits will make DAWN’s swag feel more mission-aligned and memorable.

👕 Offer apparel with flexibility and dignity

Wearability matters. Select inclusive fits, soft fabrics, and layers that work across body types and sensory preferences. Apparel should feel optional, wearable, and respectful.
