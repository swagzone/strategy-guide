✅ Implementation Tips

♿ Design for Accessibility First: Choose products with easy-open lids, lightweight materials, comfortable grips, and simple functionality so everyone can use them easily.
🧡 Prioritize Practicality: The best swag for community programs is something people reach for every day—hydration items, organizers, and notebooks are ideal.
🎨 Use Clear, High-Contrast Branding: Simple typography and high contrast colors improve readability and make products more accessible.
📦 Create Program Welcome Kits: Combine a pouch, journal, bracelet, and tumbler into a thoughtful workshop kit for participants or conference attendees.
🌍 Reflect Community Values: Swag should reinforce empowerment, inclusion, and dignity—making each item feel like part of the organization’s mission.
