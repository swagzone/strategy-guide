✅ Implementation Tips

♿ Design for Accessibility First: Choose items with easy-open lids, comfortable grips, lightweight materials, and simple usability so everyone can interact with them easily.
🧡 Prioritize Practicality: Swag that becomes part of everyday routines—drinkware, organizers, notebooks—creates lasting value and brand visibility.
🎨 Use Clear, High-Contrast Branding: Readable typography and simple decoration improve accessibility while maintaining a professional appearance.
📦 Create Program Welcome Kits: Combine multiple items into participant kits for workshops or conferences to deliver a thoughtful and organized experience.
🌍 Reflect Community Values: Ensure every product reinforces empowerment, inclusion, and community connection.
