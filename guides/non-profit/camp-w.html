Implementation Tips

🎪 Create a Merch Table Experience
Use rustic crates, mini trees, or camp signs to style your swag table like a campsite. It draws people in and encourages browsing and photos.
🎁 Offer Tiered Swag Bundles
Set donation levels that unlock bundles (e.g., $25 = bracelet + mug, $50 = cocoa kit + patch set). This increases average contribution and makes giving feel rewarding.
🔥 Use Alumni & Parent Ambassadors
Let trusted voices model or promote items. Parents wearing a “Campfire Mug” or “Legacy Patch” at the event signal value and story behind the swag.
