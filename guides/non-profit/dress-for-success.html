🎯 SWAG STRATEGY FRAMEWORK

1. Fundraising Merch Store

  • Create meaningful merch women want to wear
  • Balance mission-forward messaging with everyday appeal
  • Align designs with seasons, campaigns, or empowerment themes

2. Volunteer & Donor Recognition

  • Reward repeat supporters with tiered gifts
  • Use branded merch as gratitude and visibility tools
  • Include storytelling touches—e.g. tags with impact stats

3. Mission Amplification Gear

  • Let the merch speak the mission with slogans, symbols, or QR codes
  • Blend style and meaning—clothing and accessories with purpose
  • Incorporate eco-conscious materials to align with social values

4. Giveaway & Conference Swag

  • Choose items that foster event recall and brand affinity
  • Prioritize booth engagement and post-event use
  • Add QR codes linking to donation or volunteer sign-ups

✅ Implementation Tips

🎁 Soft Launch Fundraising Merch: Start with tees and totes at May/June events. Capture pre-orders for summer relaunch.
🧩 Tiered Recognition Program: Send small branded surprises to repeat volunteers and donors each quarter.
📱 QR-Enhanced Storytelling: Use QR codes on tags, signage, and packaging to drive traffic to impact pages.
