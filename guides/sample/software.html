🎯 SWAG STRATEGY FRAMEWORK

1. Developer Experience & Brand Affinity

  • Celebrate builders with premium, useful daily-carry gear
  • Highlight open-source spirit with collectible or hackable merch
  • Subtle branding: no “promo” vibes—just clean, dev-loved

2. Technical Team Culture & Recognition

  • Foster internal identity with “earned” gear by team or milestone
  • Use job-function branding (e.g., “Platform Ops,” “Bug Slayer”)
  • Create drops for hackathons, launches, and retrospectives

3. Enterprise Buyer & Partner Touchpoints

  • Elegant, info-rich swag for execs and IT decision-makers
  • QR-enabled gear to link directly to product demos or decks
  • Co-branding options for strategic integrations

4. Giveaway & Conference Swag

  • Make it fun, functional, and booth-worthy
  • Prioritize desk-ready gear for dev teams & tech leads
  • Add QR tags linking to sandbox, docs, or free trials
