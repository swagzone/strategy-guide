Implementation Tips

⚙️ Consistency is King
Keep logo size, colors, and brand tone consistent across every item—from notebooks to jackets—to reinforce trust and professionalism.
🕒 Time-Saving Swag Wins
Choose swag that supports the lifestyle of busy, systemized contractors—items that remove friction, add clarity, or support daily routines.
🎯 Celebrate the Wins
Use surprise merch moments (like “Freedom Kits”) to celebrate client milestones. These can boost retention, social sharing, and long-term affinity.
