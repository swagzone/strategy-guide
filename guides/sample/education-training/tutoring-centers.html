Implementation Tips

🧠 Make Swag Goal-Based
Tie swag to behavior—not attendance. Give milestone rewards for effort, growth, referrals, or attendance streaks to turn swag into motivation, not just a gift.
📦 Rotate Seasonally with Purpose
Use spring kits, exam-season relief packs, or holiday family mailers to cycle through more of the catalog while keeping content fresh and engaging.
👨‍👩‍👧 Segment by Age + Audience
Build a “student kit” and a “parent ambassador pack.” What excites a 10-year-old may bore a caregiver. Keep offerings distinct and intentional.
🧰 Brand for the Fridge & Desk
Use magnetic calendars, mini whiteboards, or even branded school supply labels to extend brand visibility into homes and workspaces.
