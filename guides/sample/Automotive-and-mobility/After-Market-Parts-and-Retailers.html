🛠 Implementation Tips

🧰 Make Swag Work as Hard as Your Customers
Choose items they’ll keep in the garage. A parts tray or branded towel won’t get tossed—it becomes part of their toolkit and builds daily affinity.
🛞 Let Loyalty Ride Shotgun
Gamify your giveaways. Give clients punch-card perks, milestone gifts, or event-exclusive merch to keep them returning and spreading the word.
🏁 Sponsor Smarter with Kits
Activate track days and events with modular swag kits. Mix in a tire gauge, branded can koozie, and microfiber towel in a branded pouch to make your name stand out.
