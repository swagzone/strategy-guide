🛠 Implementation Tips

🧽 Think Beyond the Counter

Every glove box or key return is a brand moment. Use small functional items to create surprise and show professionalism.

👨‍🔧 Let Techs Build Brand Loyalty Too

Outfit the team to take pride. Durable branded gear helps reduce turnover and raises brand perception when shared online.

📦 Build Kits, Not Just Items

Combine multiple low-cost items (towel + gauge + card) into sleek branded pouches. They’ll feel curated and increase perceived value.
