🛠 Implementation Tips

✈️ Let Loyalty Ride Along
Incentivize frequent renters and concierge referrals. Physical perks create tangible memories in an otherwise digital experience.
🧹 Treat the Car as Branded Real Estate
Design for inside-the-car branding. A seatback pouch, screen wipe, or tag stays visible and subtly reinforces your brand’s presence.
🎁 Surprise Is Powerful
Unexpected pouches in glove boxes or airport pickups win hearts. Even a low-cost branded kit feels curated when it's clean and timed right.
