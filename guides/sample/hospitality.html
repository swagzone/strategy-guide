🎯 SWAG STRATEGY FRAMEWORK

1. Guest Experience & Amenities

  • Infuse surprise + comfort into every stay with elevated branded items
  • Use sensory-focused swag that reflects location, season, or vibe
  • Make swag portable and “Instagrammable” to drive word-of-mouth

2. Staff Culture & Recognition

  • Celebrate roles with high-quality, professional gear
  • Mark anniversaries and seasonal rehires with elevated drops
  • Create unity across departments through consistent brand apparel

3. Partner & Vendor Visibility

  • Use swag to reward vendors, event planners, and high-value partners
  • Align with local makers and artisans for co-branded collections
  • Reinforce Lume’s aesthetic and hospitality values

4. Giveaway & Seasonal Campaigns

  • Create themed kits for summer, winter, or spa specials
  • Distribute at travel expos, press trips, or influencer stays
  • Use QR-enabled cards and packaging to promote bookings or loyalty programs
