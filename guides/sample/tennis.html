🗂️ Company Overview

🏢 Company Name: Tennis Association

📍 Location: Headquarters + National Reach

🏢 Type: National Sport Association (Non-Profit)

👥 Team Size: 51–200 employees

🎯 Audience: Youth players, families, fans, coaches, officials, volunteers, partners, and sponsors

🎯 Goals:
  • Promote tennis growth and accessibility nationwide
  • Support elite athlete development and grassroots participation
  • Enhance brand visibility and fan engagement
  • Foster inclusion, excellence, and national pride

🌟 SWAG STRATEGY FRAMEWORK

1. Youth & Grassroots Engagement

  • Reward participation with collectible and functional gear
  • Empower kids with practical items they’ll use and share
  • Reinforce excitement around skill-building and belonging

2. High-Performance & Team Pride

  • Equip athletes and coaches with premium gear
  • Strengthen pride through consistent, sleek branding
  • Recognize milestones with exclusive, meaningful items

3. Fan & Community Activation

  • Offer wearable, displayable swag that sparks visibility
  • Use eco-friendly and tech-savvy items to drive engagement
  • Create subtle designs fans are proud to wear daily

4. Giveaway & Event Swag

  • Provide memorable, photo-friendly handouts
  • Enhance booth experience with interactive swag
  • Design giveaways to drive traffic, digital interaction, and recall
