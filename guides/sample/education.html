🎯 SWAG STRATEGY FRAMEWORK

1. Student Life & Identity

  • Build campus connection from orientation through graduation
  • Use swag tied to student orgs, dorm life, and campus inside jokes
  • Offer limited drops and collectible swag to boost engagement

2. Faculty & Staff Belonging

  • Celebrate teaching and service milestones with professional swag
  • Unify departments with seasonal apparel or desk tools
  • Support staff identity through personalized items

3. Campus Visibility & Traditions

  • Highlight rituals and heritage with themed swag drops
  • Design event swag for gallery nights, field days, and homecoming
  • Include local partnerships (coffee shops, bookstores, artists)

4. Giveaway & Admissions Swag

  • Make open house kits useful, personal, and parent-friendly
  • Drive applications with creative QR/NFC-integrated swag
  • Focus on emotional keepsakes that inspire school pride
