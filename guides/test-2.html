🚀 Implementation Tips

🎯 Start with Essentials
Prioritize what gets worn and used daily. T-shirts, tumblers, and jackets build the strongest visibility when they live on people, not in storage closets.
📦 Build Kits by Role or Event
Bundle items into curated packs for team roles (tech, sales, admin) or for key events like client meetings or annual gatherings. It’s more memorable and intentional.
📸 Lean Into Visibility
Use swag to generate social content. Encourage staff and clients to share branded gear in the field—especially clever items like tool kits, safety stickers, or signature coasters.
🛠 Keep It Industry-Rooted
Highlight facility management DNA. Items like mini flashlights, field notebooks, or site-themed sticker packs reinforce Case’s boots-on-the-ground expertise.
