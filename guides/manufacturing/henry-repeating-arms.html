🎯 SWAG STRATEGY FRAMEWORK

1. Employee & Dealer Recognition

  • Celebrate tenure and excellence with heirloom-quality American-made goods
  • Equip dealers with branded tools that elevate the in-store experience
  • Strengthen team loyalty with limited-run, purpose-driven merch

2. Customer Loyalty & Collector Engagement

  • Surprise owners with elevated unboxing extras and mail-away rewards
  • Launch collectible swag aligned with iconic firearm models
  • Make swag worthy of display in safes, offices, and showrooms

3. Community & Cause Alignment

  • Tie merchandise to Henry’s charitable giving (e.g., “Guns for Great Causes”)
  • Support events like youth firearm safety training with relevant, durable swag
  • Use swag as a storytelling tool around heritage and responsibility

4. Giveaway & Conference Swag

  • Stick with rugged, useful gear made for outdoor, hands-on lifestyles
  • Incorporate QR-enhanced elements that link to campaign or product details
  • Create pieces that double as practical tools and brand memorabilia
