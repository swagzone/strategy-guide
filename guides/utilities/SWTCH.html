Implementation Tips

⚡ Build tiered swag levels

Use simple daily-use items like pens and notebooks for broad outreach, then step up to tech accessories and curated kits for higher-value prospects, partners, and speaking opportunities.

🎁 Package items into kits

Small gifts feel more premium when bundled with purpose. A notebook, metal pen, charging cable, and reusable cup can instantly become a thoughtful conference or client kit.

🌱 Tie merchandise to mission

Reusable and long-life products reinforce SWTCH’s position in clean energy. The more useful and sustainable the item feels, the more believable the brand connection becomes.

📱 Lean into tech

Wireless chargers, cable kits, and desk tech are especially strong because they match the brand’s energy and infrastructure story while staying useful in daily work life.

🏢 Prioritize subtle branding

For property managers, developers, and sustainability professionals, understated design often performs better than loud promotional branding. Clean placement makes items feel more gift-like.

🚗 Match the event context

At trade shows, focus on portable and immediately useful items. For partner gifting, shift toward polished desk pieces and curated kits that extend the SWTCH brand into the recipient’s daily environment.
