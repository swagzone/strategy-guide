✅ Implementation Tips

  • Closing Box Strategy: Include a “Welcome Home Bundle” with key fob, handbook, and candle for every closing—wrapped in DWH tone-on-tone branding with care card from their “Personal Builder™.”
  • Quarterly Apparel Drops: Issue seasonal apparel like tech polos or lifestyle hats to internal divisions—include different colorways or sleeve icons per city or region.
  • Realtor & Referral Loyalty Kits: Trigger premium items (like the wine opener or fleece blanket) for agents who close 3+ deals or participate in model home promotions.
  • #CareCrew Activation Packs: Equip volunteer teams with matching totes, engraved keychains, or magnetic plaques to extend brand identity into the community in a heartfelt, useful way.
