🌟 Swag Strategy Framework

1. Retail Partner Engagement

  • Highlight "cultivé au Québec" pride in everyday-use swag
  • Build shelf-level visibility with swag that educates and promotes
  • Create exclusives for top-performing dispensaries

2. Team Culture & Recognition

  • Offer outdoor-friendly swag aligned with brand lifestyle
  • Celebrate harvest milestones and employee loyalty
  • Use gear to connect staff with the product and place

3. Community & Brand Storytelling

  • Design merch around local terroir and sustainability
  • Support awareness with eco-minded giveaways
  • Collaborate with local artisans for hyperlocal impact

4. Giveaway & Conference Swag

  • Prioritize tactile, lifestyle-forward swag
  • Maximize recall through useful tools or apparel
  • Embed QR/AR links to stories or terpene maps
