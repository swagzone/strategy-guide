Regime Score: The Market “Weather Report”

4-minute read · Educational only · Not investment advice

Before looking at any idea in StockBuddy or TraderPal, we always ask: “What kind of market are we in today?”

The Regime Score answers that in plain English. It doesn’t predict anything — it simply labels the environment so you know if the market feels clean, mixed, or unstable.

What Is the Regime Score?

The Regime Score is our daily “market weather” read. Behind the scenes, our system processes volatility, strength, trend behavior, and news tone — then compresses everything into one simple label:

Hot · Warm · Icy · Cold

It’s context — not a buy or sell signal.

The Four Regimes (In Plain English)

1. Hot

A clean, supportive environment where momentum behaves well.

  • Moves follow through smoothly
  • Pullbacks feel like dips
  • Breakouts work more reliably

2. Warm

The “normal” regime — workable, mixed, but healthy overall.

  • Some setups are clean
  • Some are choppy
  • A stable but selective environment

3. Icy

A defensive environment. Trends break more easily.

  • Breakouts fail more often
  • Whipsaws increase
  • A cue to slow down

4. Cold

A risk-off environment with sharp, emotional moves.

  • Wide ranges
  • Gaps & fast reversals
  • More defense than offense

What the Regime Score Does / Doesn’t Tell You

The Regime Score is intentionally simple:

  • ✔️ Shows how friendly or hostile the market feels
  • ✔️ Highlights whether conditions are steady or shaky
  • ❌ Does not tell you what to trade
  • ❌ Does not apply to your personal risk or portfolio

It’s one layer of context — how you use it is up to you.

How It Fits Into StockBuddy & TraderPal

In StockBuddy, you get simplified weekly setups. In TraderPal, the Regime Score sits above everything, shaping expectations before you look at entries, stops, and targets.

Most users check the Regime Score label first — Hot / Warm / Icy / Cold — because it explains the “why” behind daily behavior.

Think of It Like a Weather Icon

Sunshine doesn’t guarantee a perfect day, and rain doesn’t mean you can’t go outside — but it does help you plan. The Regime Score is the same: a quick environmental read that keeps you grounded.
