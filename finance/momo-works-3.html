Is the market too stretched for longs today?

We look at breadth, RVOL, and distance from 20/50 EMA. If RVOL is elevated and breadth is narrowing, we reduce size or wait for pullbacks. Educational only.

What’s a red‑to‑green setup?

A gap‑down open that reclaims yesterday’s close, often aligning with VWAP reclaim + higher low. Torque’s pre‑market session surfaces these.

Asked by 27 users • Updated 10:05 ET

How do you place ATR stops?

We default to ~1.2–1.5× ATR(14) from entry for swings; intraday uses lower multiples based on volatility regime.
