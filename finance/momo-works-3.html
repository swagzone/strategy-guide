Is the market too stretched for longs today?

We review breadth, RVOL, and distance from 20/50 EMA. If RVOL is elevated and breadth narrows, we reduce risk or wait for pullbacks. Educational research only.

What’s a red-to-green setup?

A gap-down open that reclaims yesterday’s close, often paired with a VWAP reclaim + higher low. Torque’s pre-market view surfaces examples for study.

How do you place ATR stops?

For swings, we often reference ~1.2–1.5× ATR(14); intraday uses smaller multiples based on the volatility regime. Educational research only.
